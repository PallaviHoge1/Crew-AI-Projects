**Structured Learning Path for Financial Analysis on Implementation of AI Agents**

---

### **Beginner Level**

**Goal:** Build foundational knowledge of AI, financial analysis principles, and basic implementation concepts.

1. **Introduction to Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning**
   - **Video Course:** [AI For Everyone by Andrew Ng (Coursera)](https://www.coursera.org/learn/ai-for-everyone)
   - **Article:** ["What is Artificial Intelligence?" by TechTarget](https://searchenterpriseai.techtarget.com/definition/Artificial-Intelligence)
   - **Cheat Sheet:** [Machine Learning Cheat Sheet (DataCamp)](https://www.datacamp.com/community/blog/machine-learning-cheat-sheet)

2. **Fundamentals of Financial Analysis**
   - **Course:** [Financial Analysis for Beginners (Udemy)](https://www.udemy.com/course/financial-analysis-for-beginners/)
   - **Guide:** ["Financial Statement Analysis" by Investopedia](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/f/financialstatementanalysis.asp)
   - **Video:** [Understanding Financial Statements (Khan Academy)](https://www.khanacademy.org/economics-finance-domain/core-finance/accounting-and-financial-statements)

3. **Introduction to AI Implementation in Finance**
   - **Article:** ["How AI is transforming Financial Services" by McKinsey & Company](https://www.mckinsey.com/industries/financial-services/our-insights/ai-in-finance)
   - **Video:** [AI in Financial Services (IBM ThinkAcademy)](https://www.ibm.com/blogs/think/ai-in-financial-services/)

---

### **Intermediate Level**

**Goal:** Develop understanding of AI applications in financial analysis, data handling, and basic implementation skills.

1. **Data Handling & Basic AI Tools**
   - **Tutorial:** [Data Cleaning and Manipulation in Python (Kaggle)](https://www.kaggle.com/learn/data-cleaning)
   - **Course:** [Python for Finance (Coursera)](https://www.coursera.org/learn/python-for-finance-and-crypto)

2. **AI Techniques for Financial Forecasting**
   - **Course:** [Time Series Forecasting (Udacity)](https://www.udacity.com/course/time-series-forecasting--ud980)
   - **Tutorial:** [Stock Price Prediction using ML (DataCamp)](https://www.datacamp.com/projects/stock-price-prediction)

3. **Implementation of AI Models for Financial Analysis**
   - **Documentation:** [TensorFlow for Financial Time Series Analysis](https://www.tensorflow.org/tutorials)
   - **Article:** ["Applying Machine Learning to Financial Data" by Oâ€™Reilly Media](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/machine-learning-for/9781491935443/)

4. **Tools & Libraries**
   - **Cheatsheet:** [Pandas and NumPy for Financial Data (DataCamp)](https://www.datacamp.com/community/blog/pandas-cheat-sheet)
   - **Video:** [Using scikit-learn for Regression Analysis (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0Lt9w-BdX8k)

---

### **Advanced Level**

**Goal:** Master AI-driven financial analysis, risk assessment, and develop custom AI solutions for financial decision-making.

1. **Deep Learning for Financial Market Prediction**
   - **Course:** [Deep Learning Specialization by Andrew Ng (Coursera)](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/deep-learning)
   - **Tutorial:** [LSTM Networks for Stock Prediction (Keras)](https://blog.keras.io/using-lstm-networks-for-time-series-forecasting.html)

2. **AI for Risk Management & Fraud Detection**
   - **White Paper:** ["AI for Fraud Detection in Finance" by Deloitte](https://www2.deloitte.com/us/en/pages/financial-services/articles/ai-fraud-detection.html)
   - **Documentation:** [Implementing Risk Models with AI (QuantConnect)](https://www.quantconnect.com/docs/algorithm-reference/risk-management)

3. **Implementation & Deployment**
   - **Guide:** ["Building and Deploying AI Models in Production" by Google Cloud](https://cloud.google.com/architecture/ml-on-gcp)
   - **Toolkits:** [QuantLib & AI Integration for Derivatives Pricing](https://www.quantlib.org/documentation.shtml)

4. **Cutting-Edge Research & Case Studies**
   - **Research Paper:** ["Deep Learning in Finance" (SSRN)](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3195699)
   - **Case Study:** ["AI-driven Portfolio Optimization" by Goldman Sachs](https://www.goldmansachs.com/insights/pages/improving-portfolio-returns/file.html)
